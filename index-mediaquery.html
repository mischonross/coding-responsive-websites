A little bit about me.

I've made my home base in Wakanda. I am currently an empty nester and LOVING IT! I have sons who are grown up and living all over the world. My journey into tech started in the 7th grade in a computer class. I quickly found that I loved software and figuring out how it worked. I started with the basics (Word, Excel, Access and eventually SQL). I've always dabbled in some sort of web building. I was fascinated with DOS 3.1 and that foundation led me to want to learn a lot of other things. I am a true believer in life-long learning. I want to be in a position where I build a business as a freelancer and show off all the wonderful skills I learned at Skillcrush!

Curiosity about software, computers & the web, opened a door that could never be shut. When I learned how to build my first website, I realized I found something that gave me the freedom & versatility I was looking for in my work. Now I've made a full switch to front-end development, where I can use my organization skills and eye for detail to write clean, elegant code. I am looking forward to the day where I have a steady client base and freelancing full-time.
